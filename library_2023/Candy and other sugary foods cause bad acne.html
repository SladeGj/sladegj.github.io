Candy and other sugary foods cause bad acne

Sugar definitely doesn’t help your acne, but it also won’t cause it. As long as you’re not consuming sweet snacks by the pound, sugar and candy will likely have little effect on your skin. The amount of acne you have is mainly based on your genetics and hormones.

Sources and literature

Claudel J. P. et al. Acne and nutrition: hypotheses, myths and facts //Journal of the European Academy of Dermatology and Venereology. – 2018. – Т. 32. – №. 10. – С. 1631-1637.
