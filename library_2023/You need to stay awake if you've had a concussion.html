You need to stay awake if you've had a concussion

Back in the day, the only way to test brain function in a concussed person was to wake them every hour and gauge their level of responsiveness. Now, CT, MRI and PET scans can tell us how alert and oriented a patient is. In fact, we now know that the brain recovers more quickly when it gets lots of rest
